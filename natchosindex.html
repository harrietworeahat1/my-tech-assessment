directions

1. Preheat oven to 110 degrees C and line the baking tray with baking paper.

2. Beat the egg white until stiff peaks form.

3. Gradually add the sugar, beating all the time until it is thick and glossy.

4. Fill a piping bag with the mixture and pipe small twirls on the tray, using a teaspoon to press down any peaks on the meringues.

5. Bake for 20-30mins until meringues are dry and leave the baking paper easily.
