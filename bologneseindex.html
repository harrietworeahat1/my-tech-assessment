Ingredients

3 tbsp olive oil 300g beef mince 200g pork mince one oninon ( or 2 large shallots), finely chopped 2-3 garlic cloves,crushed 500g passata 1 tbsp tomato pur√©e 100ml red wine (optional) 1 tsp dried oregano 400g spaghetti parmesan, finely grated (as much as you like for on top) a few basil leaves

steps

Step 1: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Brown the beef, then transfer to a dish. Repeat with 1 tablespoon of oil and the pork. Add the pork to the dish with the beef. Return the pan to the heat with the remaining oil. Reduce the heat and cook the shallots for 8-10 minutes until very soft, then add the garlic. Return the meat to the pan and add the passata, tomato paste, wine, and oregano. Stir everything together, cover, and simmer over low heat for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Stir half of the parmesan into the Bolognese sauce. Add a spoonful of pasta water to the sauce if it's too thick, then drain the spaghetti. For better flavor, toss the pasta with the sauce, ensuring it's well coated, and season well. Alternatively, serve the sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with the remaining parmesan and a few basil leaves.
