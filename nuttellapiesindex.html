Nutrition facts

1 hand pie: 190 calories, 10g fat. This recipe makes 9 servings. the total time for this is around 30 minutes.

Ingredients required

1 large egg 1 tablespoon water 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed 3 tablespoons Nutella 1 to 2 teaspoons grated orange zest

Icing: 1/3 cup confectioners' sugar 1/2 teaspoon orange juice 1/8 teaspoon grated orange zest Additional Nutella, optional

directions

Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a small bowl, whisk egg with water. Unfold puff pastry; cut into 9 squares. Place 1 teaspoon Nutella in the center of each square; sprinkle with orange zest. Brush edges of pastry with egg mixture. Fold 1 corner over filling to form a triangle; press edges to seal. Transfer to an ungreased baking sheet. Bake until pastry is golden brown and cooked through, 17-20 minutes. Cool slightly. In a small bowl, mix confectioners' sugar, orange juice, and orange zest; drizzle over pies. If desired, warm additional Nutella in a microwave and drizzle over tops.
