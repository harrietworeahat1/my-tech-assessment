(Prep 20 min, Cook 25 min)

Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Free From Gluten plain flour

- 2 Free-range eggs

- 1 cup Free From Gluten breadcrumbs

- 1/2 bunch Continental parsley, leaves picked, finely chopped

- 500 g Chicken breast fillets, cut into 3cm pieces

- 5 ml Olive oil cooking spray

- 1/2 cup Whole egg mayonnaise

- 1 Tbsp Sriracha

- 1 Lemon, cut into wedges, to serve

Directions

1. Preheat a 7L air fryer to 200Â°C for 3 minutes.

2. Meanwhile, place flour on a plate and whisk egg in a shallow bowl.

3. Combine breadcrumbs and parsley on a separate plate.

4. Working with one piece of chicken at a time, toss in flour, then dip in egg and coat in breadcrumb mixture. Place on a baking tray. Repeat with remaining chicken, flour, egg, and breadcrumb mixture.

5. Place half the chicken, in a single layer, in the air fryer basket. Spray with oil. Slide pan and basket into air fryer. Set timer for 12 minutes. Cook, turning chicken halfway through cooking, or until golden and cooked through. Repeat process with remaining chicken.

6. Meanwhile, combine mayonnaise with sriracha in a small bowl. Season with pepper. Garnish chicken with extra parsley, then serve with mayonnaise mixture and lemon.
