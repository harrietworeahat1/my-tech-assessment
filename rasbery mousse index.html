Raspberry Mousse

ingredients

• 12 oz or 1 1/2 C frozen raspberries + 1/4 C water • 1/2 cup sugar • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream, make sure it's really cold • 4 sheets + 1 cup cold water to soften

Directions

1. Soak gelatine in 1 cup cold water to soften. Set aside.

2. In a small saucepan, add raspberries, sugar, and 1/4 cup water. Heat for about 5 minutes until berries soften.

3. Blend the mixture and strain through a sieve to remove any seeds. Put the mixture back into the saucepan.

4. Add softened gelatine to the saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature (70-80 degrees F).

5. In a large bowl, whip cream to stiff peaks. Be careful not to over whip.

6. Once the berry mixture is adequately cooled, carefully fold it into the whipped cream. Pour into serving glasses.

7. Chill before serving.
