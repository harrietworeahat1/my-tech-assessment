Ingredients

2 lb / 1 kg potatoes, peeled (4 large ones) (Note 1)

3 tbsp olive oil

Seasoning

1 1/2 tsp paprika

1 tsp garlic powder

1/2 tsp onion powder (or sub with more garlic powder)

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220째C/430째F (200째C/390째F fan/convection)

2. Mix the Seasoning ingredients.

3. Cut the potatoes in half lengthwise, then cut each half into 4 "wedges" (so you get 8 pieces from each potato). If your potato is small/medium, just cut into 6 wedges.

4. Place potatoes in a large bowl, drizzle over oil and seasoning. Toss with hands.

5. Spread out on tray, ensuring cut side lies flat on the tray.

6. Bake for 35 to 45 minutes, turning once, or until dark golden brown and crispy.

7. Serve immediately!

Dipping sauces: Classic way is with sour cream and sweet chilli sauce. Otherwise, try tomato sauce, BBQ sauce, ranch, aioli ect.
