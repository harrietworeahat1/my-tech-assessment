Nutrition facts

There are 768 Calories This recipe makes 6 servings (prep time: 10 mins cooking time: 15 mins)

Ingredients

1 Tbsp oil

1 red onion, chopped

1 clove garlic, chopped

500 g beef mince

35 g mild taco seasoning

400 g can red kidney beans, drained

200 g can diced tomatoes

220 g corn chips

1 cup grated cheese

125 g sour cream

1 tomato, chopped, to serve

Chopped parsley (optional)

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (160°C fan-forced).

2. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 3 minutes or until softened. Add the beef mince and cook until browned all over. Stir through the taco seasoning and cook for a further 2 minutes.

3. Add the kidney beans and diced tomatoes to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Place the corn chips in a large baking dish. Pour the mince mixture over the top and sprinkle with grated cheese.

5. Bake in the oven until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

6. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream, a sprinkle of chopped tomato, and a garnish of parsley.

Tip: Try using premium mince which has less fat. You can also add grated carrot, mushrooms, or capsicum for extra vegetables.
